Headline: FBI admits forging evidence in 95% cases, death penalty as well
Source: Pravda
Country: Russia

Post:

<p>The US legal system faces the biggest forensic scandal related to hair and bite-mark analysis.</p>

<p><strong>National Association of Criminal Defense Lawyers claimed that the FBI Laboratory's microscopic hair comparison unit fabricated forensic matches in order to meet the prosecutors' demands.</strong></p>

<p>Under the investigation conducted together with the Innocence Project, the case appears in 95 % of the 268 trials.</p>

<p>Among the given suspects, <strong>32 people were sentenced to death.</strong></p>

<p>Four accused persons have been already justified.</p>

<p>Peter Neufeld, co-founder of the Innocence Project, claimed, that "the FBI's three-decade use of microscopic hair analysis to incriminate defendants was a complete disaster."</p>
